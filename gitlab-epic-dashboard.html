How to Use EpicDash (Group Mode)

1. Paste your Group URL (e.g. https://gitlab.com/gitlab-org)

2. Use a Personal Access Token with read_api scope

3. Filters (Labels, Assignees, Milestones) now work across ALL epics

4. Estimates & time tracking are fully loaded and visible

Everything runs in your browser – no data is sent anywhere else.
